VìSD (Vì Sustainable Development)

About Us

The company integrates SAAS software to monitor ESG indicators, support risk management, propose solutions, conduct internal training, and provide ESG reports for businesses in need.

Mission

Accompanying Vietnamese businesses on their journey of sustainable transformation through proposals and contributions on ESG performance, helping to enhance the country's economic value.

Vission

Become a pioneering unit, the preferred choice for businesses in the field of ESG consulting services in Vietnam. Contributing to sustainable development, promoting green transformation, and international integration.

Main Service

Comprehensive ESG strategy consulting and development is a service that provides the implementation process from basic steps: current status assessment, defining sustainability goals, to complex steps: benchmarking the current situation and developing a plan suitable for business operations. The service price ranges from 80 million to over 150 million VND depending on the project's scale and complexity.

Prepare ESG reports according to international standards (GRI, SASB, TCFD), provide services to support businesses in data collection and standardize information according to domestic and international reporting frameworks to meet legal, fundraising, and service valuation needs. Basic reports (GRI Score, ESG Ranking) range from 30 to 60 million, while specialized reports (GRI Comprehensive + SASB) range from 60 to 150 million, depending on the industry and organizational size.

The VìSD SaaS Platform & AI Dashboard Analysis platform, which connects online to the VISD website, allows businesses to track ESG indicators in real-time and receive alerts for exceeding thresholds. Basic features include: ESG Dashboard, automatic alerts for exceeding thresholds, and a storage system for exporting reports at the time set by the company. The application calculates prices annually and has 3 selection criteria: Basic (60 million), Advanced (99 million), and Enterprise + Adjustment Consulting (89 - 149 million).

b) Additional services

Regarding the two additional services, including ESG knowledge training for businesses. VISD is responsible for providing knowledge training through online courses for personnel who need it, and for answering questions about ESG-related information: the current situation, ESG planning, knowledge of the GRI, FSC standards, and what constitutes Greenwashing violations. Employee courses generally range from 6 million to 10 million, and intensive courses (supporting certification registration) from 20-30 million.

Finally, the ES Risk Assessment, which analyzes the risks of investment projects and special infrastructure works, is particularly important to meet the requirements of international banks (IFC, ADB) when borrowing funds, with prices ranging from 70 to 150 million, depending on the scale and implementation time.
